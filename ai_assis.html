// CatHacks Project

AI Student Assistant

An intelligent AI-powered study assistant built during CatHacks to help students summarize notes, ask questions, organize learning resources, and improve productivity through conversational AI.

View Devpost Explore Project

// Inspiration

Built to make studying faster and smarter.

We wanted to create an AI platform capable of helping students interact with their study materials naturally. Instead of searching endlessly through PDFs and notes, students can simply ask questions and receive intelligent, contextual answers instantly.

AI Learning

Conversational AI assistance powered by modern language models.

Document Analysis

Upload notes and PDFs to summarize and interact with learning materials.

// Features

What the assistant can do.

AI Chat

Ask questions naturally and receive intelligent, contextual responses.

Study Summaries

Quickly generate concise summaries from large documents and notes.

Smart Explanations

Break down difficult concepts into simpler, understandable explanations.

Productivity

Reduce study time and improve efficiency with AI-assisted learning.
